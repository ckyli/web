Why test for Mycoplasma?

Importance of Mycoplasma testing:

    It is always good practice to check your cell cultures on a regular basis for Mycoplasma infection. Mycoplasma contamination could be devastating to your cell culture facility. If not detected early, this can result in:

    • Expensive costs
    • Wasted time and effort
    • Cellular changes, e.g. cell growth, chromosome aberrations

Test procedure:

The MycoSensor PCR Assay Kit (Agilent Technologies #302109) will be used to detect Mycoplasma infections in your cell culture samples. This will take around approximately 2 hours.

The MycoSensor PCR Assay Kit detects the following 8 species:

  • M. arginini
  • M. fermantas
  • M. hominis
  • M. hyorhinis
  • M. pirum
  • M. salivarium
  • M. orale
  • M. laidlawi

Although detection of other, less common species has not been specifically tested, the assay may detect the presence of other related species.
